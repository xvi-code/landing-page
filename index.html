Adopt, don't shop!
Each month, there are as many as 200 abandoned pets at animal shelters. There are approximately 7,000 stray dogs and 13,000 stray cats in Singapore! When you adopt an animal from a shelter, you are not only giving that animal a second chance, but also helping us save more lives by freeing up the shelter space for another adoptable animal.
